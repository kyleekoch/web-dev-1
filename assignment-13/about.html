About Me

Hi everyone! My name is Kylee Koch and I am a senior media arts major at the University of Montana.
I have a passion for art and with any free time that I have I am most likely drawing or working on a project for someone.
I specialize in hand drawing with pen and pencil, and I also have developed a newfound love for making pieces on photoshop and illustrator.
If you would like to see some of my work head to the gallery page and check it out, and if you are interested in any of my work feel free to
head to the contact page and get my information to get in contact with me. Thanks for visiting my page, I hope you enjoy!
